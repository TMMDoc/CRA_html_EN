This SR requires the implementation of regular backups of critical system data, configurations, and software to ensure recovery in case of failure, cyberattacks, or data corruption. Requirement Enhancements (REs) under SR 7.3: SR 7.3 RE 1 – Backup integrity verification (ensuring backups are not corrupted or tampered with). SR 7.3 RE 2 – Protection of backup information (ensuring backups are stored securely and access is restricted). SR 7.3 RE 3 – Backup recovery testing (periodic testing to confirm backups can be successfully restored).

 Redundancy and Failover: Ensure system availability through redundancy, failover mechanisms, and robust backup processes.

 Disaster Recovery Plan: Develop and test a disaster recovery plan to restore critical systems and operations after an incident.

 Regular Patching: Establish a patch management process to ensure that all systems are regularly updated with security patches.

 Vulnerability Scanning: Conduct regular vulnerability scans to identify and remediate security weaknesses in the system.
