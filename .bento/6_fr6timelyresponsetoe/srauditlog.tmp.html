This SR requires that the system generate audit logs for security-relevant events, including authentication attempts, configuration changes, and security incidents. Additionally, there are Requirement Enhancements (REs) under SR 6.1 that further strengthen log management:

SR 6.1 RE 1 – Protection of audit information (ensuring logs cannot be modified or deleted by unauthorized users).

This enhancement requires that audit logs be protected from modification or deletion by unauthorized users.

Using write-once, read-many (WORM) storage or similar tamper-proof logging mechanisms meets this requirement.

SR 6.1 RE 2 – Audit storage capacity (ensuring sufficient storage for logs).

SR 6.1 RE 3 – Audit log accessibility (allowing secure access for authorized personnel).
